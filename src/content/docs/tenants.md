# Tenants

Tenants are the people renting your property or units.

## Add a Tenant

### From the Property Page

1. Open a property
2. In the **"Tenants"** card, click **"Add Tenant"**
3. Fill in:
   - **First Name:** Required
   - **Last Name:** Required
   - **Preferred Name:** Optional (how they like to be called)
   - **ID Card Number:** Optional
   - **Phone:** Required
   - **Unit:** Optional - select which unit they occupy
   - **Status:** Active or Inactive
4. Click **"Add"**

### From the Tenants Page

1. Go to **"Tenants"** in the navigation
2. Select which property
3. Click **"Add Tenant"**
4. Follow the same steps above

## View Tenant Details

Click any tenant's name to see:

- Contact information
- Assigned unit
- Rent payment history

## Edit a Tenant

1. Open the tenant details page
2. Click **"Edit"**
3. Update any information
4. Click **"Save"**

## Delete a Tenant

1. Open the tenant details page
2. Click **"Delete Tenant"**
3. Confirm the deletion

> **Note:** Deleting a tenant also deletes all their rent entries. Consider marking them as "Inactive" instead to preserve history.
