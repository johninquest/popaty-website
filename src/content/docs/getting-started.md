# Getting Started

Welcome to Popaty! This guide will help you get started with managing your properties.

## Signing In

1. Navigate to the Popaty application
2. Click **"Sign in with Google"**
3. Select your Google account
4. Grant the necessary permissions

**Note:** You must use a Gmail account to sign in.


## Dashboard Overview

After signing in, you'll see your dashboard with two main sections:

### My Properties

Properties you own and have full control over.

### Shared With Me

Properties that others have shared with you (view or manage access).

## Quick Actions

From the dashboard, you can:

- **Create a new property** - Click the "New Property" button
- **View property details** - Click on any property card
- **Access shared properties** - Switch to the "Shared With Me" tab
- **Manage your profile** - Click on your profile icon


## Understanding Property Structure

Each property in Popaty follows this hierarchy:

**Property** → **Units** → **Tenants** → **Rent & Expenses**

- **Property:** The building or complex you manage
- **Unit:** Individual apartments, rooms, or spaces
- **Tenant:** People renting the units
- **Rent/Expenses:** Financial transactions for each tenant
