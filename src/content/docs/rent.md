# Rent Entries

This is where you record rent payments from tenants.

## Record a Rent Payment

### Quick Method

1. Click **"Record Rent"** from the dashboard
2. Select property, then tenant
3. Enter:
   - **Amount:** How much they paid
   - **Payment Date:** When they paid
   - **Rent Month:** Which month this payment covers (e.g., "2025-01" for January 2025)
   - **Payment Method:** Cash, bank transfer, mobile money, etc.
   - **Notes:** Optional additional information
4. Click **"Record Payment"**

### From Property Page

1. Open a property
2. Click the **"Rent Entries"** tab
3. Click **"Record Rent"**
4. Tenant is pre-filtered to this property
5. Fill in the payment details
6. Click **"Record Payment"**

1. Click on any rent entry
2. Click **"Edit"**
3. Update any field
4. Click **"Save Changes"**

## Delete a Rent Entry
